Our Story

Founded by avid travellers, Ghumoowithus was born out of a desire to share the magic of exploration with the world. We believe travel is more than just visiting a place — it’s about immersing yourself in new cultures, forging connections and creating memories that last a lifetime. Our team scouts destinations globally to hand‑pick experiences that delight both first‑time adventurers and seasoned wanderers.

From the snow‑capped Himalayas to the sun‑drenched beaches of Goa, we curate journeys that combine iconic sights with hidden gems. Whether you’re looking for a solo retreat, a family getaway or a group expedition, our mission is to take care of all the details so you can focus on the experience.
